Explore Cantor College: World-Class Facilities for Your Success

At Cantor College, we are committed to providing our students with the best possible environment to learn, create, and innovate. Our state-of-the-art facilities are designed to support your academic journey and help you thrive in your chosen field. Whether you’re studying computing, design, or technology, our campus offers everything you need to excel.

Our Facilities

  • Advanced Computing Labs

    Equipped with the latest hardware/software, accessible 24/7 for programming, cybersecurity, and data science projects.

  • Design Studios

    High-end tools for digital media, product design, and collaboration — fully outfitted for professional-grade output.

  • Innovation and Makerspace

    3D printers, CNC machines, and prototyping tools to bring your ideas to life in a hands-on learning environment.

  • Technology Sandbox

    A place to explore VR, AR, and AI technologies with access to cutting-edge devices and experimental software.

  • Collaborative Learning Spaces

    Flexible workspaces with smartboards, conferencing tools, and group-friendly layouts to support team projects.

  • Library and Resource Centre

    Comprehensive digital and physical resources, quiet study areas, and research tools tailored to computing and design fields.

  • Student Hub

    Café, lounge, and club space — the social heart of campus for unwinding, connecting, and staying involved.

  • Career and Development Centre

    Support for your professional journey, including career counseling, resume help, and employer networking events.

  • Fitness and Wellness Centre

    Gym, exercise studios, wellness classes, and counseling services to support your mental and physical well-being.

  • On-Campus Housing

    Modern student accommodation with high-speed internet, study lounges, and a vibrant living community.

Experience Cantor College

Our facilities are built to help you succeed — academically, socially, and professionally. Explore, engage, and make the most of your time with us.
