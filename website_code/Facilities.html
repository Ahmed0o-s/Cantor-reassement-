Welcome to Our Facilities

The College is located in an attractive and pleasantly refurbished building. The building houses computing laboratories and lecture/tutorial rooms. It has its own catering facilities and student work areas. There is also a car park to the back of the building.

Our building spans 9500m², accommodates over 240 staff, and provides teaching space for more than 1600 students.

Facilities include:

  • Wi-Fi technology
  • Pool teaching rooms (25–80 students)
  • Specialist faculty facilities
  • Double-height lecture theatre
  • Three-storey glass atrium
  • Meeting rooms
  • Office accommodation
  • Specialist IT facilities
  • Reception area
  • Catering outlet
  • Disabled parking
  • Cycle racks
  • Gallery
